What is a Fitness Workout?


A fitness workout is a planned session of physical activity designed to improve overall health, strength, endurance, flexibility, and body composition. It combines various exercises that target different areas of the body and fitness goals.



Benefits of Regular Workouts

  • Increased energy and focus
  • Improved sleep and mood
  • Stronger immune system
  • Long-term weight management
  • Boosted confidence and self-esteem
