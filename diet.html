Diet Plans for Different Goals

General Fitness
Focus: Balanced nutrients, steady energy, consistency.
Diet:
Breakfast: Oats with fruits and nuts
Mid-morning Snack: Greek yogurt or fruit
Lunch: Brown rice, dal, mixed veggies, salad
Evening Snack: Handful of almonds, green tea
Dinner: Grilled paneer/tofu with steamed veggies
Post-dinner: Herbal tea
Strength Goal
Focus: Protein-rich, calorie surplus, recovery-focused.
Sample Diet:
Breakfast: Eggs, multigrain toast, avocado
Snack: Protein shake + banana
Lunch: Chicken breast, quinoa, broccoli
Snack:Peanut butter toast or trail mix
Dinner: Grilled salmon, sweet potato, spinach
Before bed: Cottage cheese
Weight Loss / Fat Loss
Focus: Calorie deficit, high protein, fiber-rich.
Sample Diet:
Breakfast: Smoothie (spinach, berries, protein powder, almond milk)
Snack: Boiled eggs or fruit
Lunch: Moong dal chilla with green chutney + salad
Snack: Roasted makhana
Dinner: Soup + sauteed veggies
Hydration: 3-4 L water/day
Flexibility & Mobility
Focus:Anti-inflammatory foods, hydration, omega-3.
Sample Diet:
Breakfast: Chia pudding with walnuts and flaxseeds
Lunch: Lentil soup + leafy greens
Dinner: Stir-fried tofu + turmeric tea
Snacks: Fruits, coconut water
Mental & Habitual
Focus: Consistent timing, mindful eating, gut health.
Sample Diet:
Fermented foods: Curd, kefir, sauerkraut
Balanced meals: Good fat, complex carbs, proteins
Mindful snacks: Dark chocolate, green tea
Morning: Warm lemon water
Evening: Chamomile tea
